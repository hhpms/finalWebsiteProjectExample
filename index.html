About This Class

In Computer Science Discoveries, we learned how computer science helps people solve problems and create new tools. We explored what computers are, how they process information, and how software connects to hardware. We practiced digital citizenship so we could use technology safely, respectfully, and responsibly. We also built interactive animations and games using GameLab, learned to code hardware with micro:bits, and explored how data and AI can be used to help (and sometimes mislead) people. Finally, we learned the basics of web development and created our own websites to share our work.

What Our Final Websites Include

Each student’s final website includes a reflection on what they learned in this class, a career profile that explains how a chosen job uses computer science, and a profile of a historical figure who made important contributions to computer science. Each student also includes their final interactive game project which they coded in JavaScript with code.org's GameLab. These pages show both our creativity and our growing skills as problem-solvers, programmers, and responsible digital citizens.
